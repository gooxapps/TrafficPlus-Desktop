# TrafficPlus Features List

## User Experience & Core Features
1. **Campaign Analytics Dashboard**: Detailed metrics for each campaign (bounce rate, time spent, devices, locations, etc.)
2. **Campaign Targeting**: More targeting options (countries, devices, interests, time of day)
3. **Campaign A/B Testing**: Test multiple versions of campaign landing pages or settings
4. **Surf Session Rewards**: Bonus credits for longer surf sessions
5. **Dark/Light Mode**: Toggle between light and dark themes
6. **User Profiles**: Customizable user profile with avatar, bio, etc.
7. **Notification Center**: In-app notifications for credit updates, campaign status, referrals, etc.
8. **Contact List**: Manage saved contacts for newsletters/updates
9. **Saved Campaigns**: Quick access to frequently used campaign settings
10. **Multi-Tab Surfing**: Open multiple surf tabs with a single click

## Referral & Community
11. **Referral Leaderboard**: Top referrers with prizes/bonuses
12. **Referral Analytics**: Track how many referrals you've made, their activity, etc.
13. **Community Forum**: Users can ask questions, share tips, etc.
14. **Gamification Levels/Points**: Levels based on credits earned, with rewards at each level
15. **Badges/Achievements**: Collect badges for hitting milestones (e.g., "First Campaign", "100 Referrals")

## Premium/Monetization
16. **Subscription Tiers**: Premium plans with more features (unlimited campaigns, higher daily limits, better targeting, etc.)
17. **Credit Packages**: Buy additional credits in bulk with discounts
18. **Ad Revenue Sharing**: Share revenue from ads shown during surf sessions
19. **White Label Campaigns**: Premium users can brand their campaigns

## Admin & Moderation
20. **Admin Dashboard**: Manage users, campaigns, moderate content
21. **Campaign Approval Queue**: Review campaigns before they go live
22. **Fraud Detection**: Detect and prevent fake surf sessions or spam campaigns
23. **User Ban/Moderation Tools**: Manage problematic users

## Integrations
24. **Google Analytics Integration**: Connect GA for deeper campaign insights
25. **Social Media Sharing**: Share referral links or campaign updates to social media
26. **Email Newsletters**: Automated newsletters with tips, updates, and special offers
27. **API Access**: Allow developers to build on top of TrafficPlus

## Mobile & Offline
28. **Mobile App (PWA)**: Make the app installable on mobile devices
29. **Offline Surfing**: Save credits offline and sync when back online
